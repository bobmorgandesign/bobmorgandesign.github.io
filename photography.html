ABOUT

Bob Morgan does his own photography or employs the services of top local photographers depending on job requirements. The choice depends upon the photographer's strengths relative to desired design aesthetic which is paramount. In addition, Bob is an avid enthusiast who is constantly taking shots just for the pure joy of making images. Click on the images to see a portfolio of professional shots (left) and personal shots (right) that demonstrate his personal photographic style and the level of ability that he brings to a project.
