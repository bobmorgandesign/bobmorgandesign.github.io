ABOUT

BOB MORGAN

has had a successful career as a Saint John NB Canada based Art Director and Graphic Designer for more than thirty years. He spent twenty-six years as General Manager and Art Director for Fulton Graphics Limited whose major clients included J.D. Irving, Limited, Irving Oil Limited, McCain Foods, Baxter Dairies, Saint John Shipbuilding, NB Department of Commerce and Development, CAA Atlantic, CAA Ontario, Irving Paper, Irving Pulp & Paper, Enterprise Saint John, The City of Saint John, Human Resources Assoc. of NB, Waterfront Development Partnership, Canadian Assoc. of Emergency Physicians, etc.

Following this Bob was Design Director for New Brunswick's major newspaper the Telegraph-Journal. His principal role was to implement a major redesign of the publication by consultant Lucie Lacava of Montreal. This was successfully

accomplished and the team was recognized by two prestigious Society for News Design (Rhode Island) Awards of Excellence in March 2007.

Since forming his own design company in 2007 Bob does work for a growing number of clients including Commercial Properties Limited, The City of Saint John, Ketchum Realty, Liquor Lodge Inc., Bentley Crossing, Chiasson Design, Revolution Strategy, Goose Lane Editions, Peter Buckland Gallery, Fabinex, etc. Working closely with associated company Chiasson Design (formerly Fulton Graphics) he also continues to work on projects for CAA Atlantic,  J.D. Irving, Limited and other clients. As of 2017 both Bob Morgan Design and Chiasson Design are now located in Montreal Canada where they continue to serve their clients from across Canada.
